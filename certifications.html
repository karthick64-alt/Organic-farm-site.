Our Certifications

USDA Organic Certified

Our farm is USDA Organic certified, meaning we meet strict standards for organic production, including no synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or GMOs.

Certification #: OF-12345
Valid Until: December 2025

Non-GMO Project Verified

All our products are verified by the Non-GMO Project, ensuring they contain no genetically modified organisms.

Verification #: NGM-67890

Certified Sustainable

We're certified by the Sustainable Agriculture Network for our commitment to environmental protection and social responsibility.

Local Farm Recognition

Winner of "Local Farm of the Year 2023" by the Regional Agriculture Association for excellence in organic farming practices.

Quality Assurance

Lab Testing

We conduct regular laboratory testing to ensure our produce meets the highest standards for:

  • Pesticide residue (must be below detectable levels)
  • Nutritional content
  • Heavy metals and contaminants
  • Microbiological safety

Test results are available upon request and are maintained on file for all products.

Traceability

Every product can be traced back to its source. Our traceability system includes:

  • Field of origin
  • Harvest date
  • Batch numbers
  • Processing and packaging information

Scan the QR code on your product packaging to access detailed traceability information.

QR Code Traceability

Each product package includes a unique QR code that provides:

  • Product origin and farm location
  • Harvest and packaging dates
  • Certification verification
  • Lab test results
  • Storage and preparation tips

Scan with your smartphone to access product information
