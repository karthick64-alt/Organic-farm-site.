Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Our Satisfaction Guarantee

At Organic Farm, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, we'll make it right.

Product Quality Issues

If you receive produce that is damaged, spoiled, or doesn't meet our quality standards, please contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We will:

  • Provide a full refund for the affected items
  • Replace the items on your next order at no charge
  • Or credit your account for future purchases

Order Cancellations

Before Shipment

You may cancel your order at any time before it has been processed and shipped. To cancel, contact us by phone or email as soon as possible. You will receive a full refund.

After Shipment

Once your order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled. However, you may return eligible items according to our return policy.

Returns

Due to the perishable nature of our products, we generally do not accept returns of fresh produce. However, we will provide refunds or replacements for items that don't meet our quality standards.

For non-perishable items (such as packaged goods), returns may be accepted within 14 days of purchase, provided items are unopened and in original packaging.

Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings or by contacting us. Cancellations will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will continue to receive deliveries until the end of your paid period.

Refunds for subscription fees are prorated based on the remaining deliveries in your billing cycle.

Refund Processing

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5-10 business days of approval. Processing times may vary depending on your financial institution.

Farm Visits and Workshops

Cancellations made at least 48 hours before a scheduled farm visit or workshop will receive a full refund. Cancellations made less than 48 hours in advance may receive a 50% refund or credit toward a future visit.

We reserve the right to cancel or reschedule events due to weather or other circumstances beyond our control. In such cases, you will receive a full refund or the option to reschedule.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us:

  • Email: support@organicfarm.com
  • Phone: (555) 123-4567
  • Include your order number and reason for the refund request

Exceptions

Custom orders, special arrangements, and bulk orders may have different refund policies. These will be communicated at the time of order.
